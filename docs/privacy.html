Privacy policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This policy explains what Reigncraft collects when you play the Reddit Devvit game, and why.

Who we are

Reigncraft is an independent game running on Reddit’s developer platform (Devvit). The operator is the app developer reachable as u/IanoMasuel.

What we collect

  • Reddit identity. Reddit provides the app with the information Devvit normally exposes for installed apps (such as your Reddit username / account id) so multiplayer and saves can work.
  • Gameplay data. Things like position, inventory, health, room membership, and similar game state may be stored so you can keep playing and so other players in a room can see you.
  • Technical logs. Devvit and our backends may keep short-lived logs for debugging crashes, abuse, or outages.

Where data goes

Game data may be stored in Reddit Devvit services (including Redis where enabled) and in our configured backend (Convex). If optional features are turned on, the app may call other allowlisted HTTP APIs (for example media or AI helpers). We do not sell your personal information.

Why we use it

  • Run the game and multiplayer rooms
  • Save progress and settings
  • Fix bugs and stop abuse
  • Improve balance and stability during soft release

Sharing

Other players in your room can see in-game presence (name/avatar-style display and gameplay state the mode exposes). Platform providers (Reddit, Convex, and any optional API you trigger) process data as needed to provide their service. We do not run ads against your Reigncraft data.

Retention

Soft-release data may be cleared when we reset test worlds or remove unused installs. We keep data only as long as it is useful to run or debug the game, unless a longer period is required by the platform.

Children

Reigncraft follows Reddit’s age rules. Do not use the app if you are not allowed to use Reddit.

Your choices

You can stop playing and uninstall the app from communities you moderate, or leave rooms you joined. For deletion requests about game data we control, contact u/IanoMasuel. Reddit account data is handled under Reddit’s own privacy policy.

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change when we do.

Contact

Privacy questions: u/IanoMasuel or the GitHub repo.

This page is a practical summary for Devvit publishing, not formal legal advice.
